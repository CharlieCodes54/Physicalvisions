Your Coach

Cody Carrier

NASM CPT · NASM Sport Nutrition · RAD-T Mental Health Technician · 12 Years Experience

Cody didn't start in a gym. He started in a treatment center — his first clients were freshly sober men and women with no desire to work out. Getting them to move wasn't a fitness challenge. It was a mental health intervention.

What he witnessed changed everything. The people who showed up, who moved, who started eating with intention — they healed differently. That experience shaped everything Cody has done since.

Over the next 12 years, he trained cancer patients and people with autoimmune disorders, led hundreds of group coaching sessions connecting movement to recovery, and developed a methodology proven across thousands of conversations: talk to people personally, help them find one small thing to add, and watch their lives change.

After a motor vehicle accident in 2025, Cody moved his practice fully online. The weekly call is that same methodology — now available to anyone who shows up.
