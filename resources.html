Get Set Up

Four steps, about fifteen minutes

01

Find your baseline with NASM's calculator

NASM's free calorie calculator gives you a starting daily number based on your age, weight, activity level, and goal. It's not a diet, just a number worth having before you start logging anything.

Open the Calculator
02

Create a free MyFitnessPal account

MyFitnessPal is where you'll log meals day to day. The free account is all you need to get started, no premium subscription required.

Create Your Account
03

Turn on your phone's Health app

If you're on iPhone, open the Health app that's already installed and make sure it's tracking steps and activity. On Android, your phone's own health app works the same way. Nothing new to download, just make sure it's switched on.

04

Get access to Trainerize — $50/month

This is what lets Cody see your logs and coach you live on the Thursday call. Once you're in, connect MyFitnessPal and your phone's health data inside the app so everything shows up in one place.

Get Access to Trainerize
